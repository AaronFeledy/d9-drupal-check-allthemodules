Partner Link

## Requirements

* This module is available only for Drupal 8 websites.

## Installation

* No dependencies. Just enable the module.

## Configuration

* Create your website partners into the taxonomy 'Partners' (partner_link__partners)
* You can now visit the page /partners which lists all your partners
* You also can place the block 'Partner Link' into one of your region. The block will list your partners that have been marked as listed into the 'Partner Link' block.
