INTRODUCTION
------------

Story Chief is a brand new tool that lets you push your content to different
publishing channels.
It has a universal editor that lets you create beautiful stories with ease,
making them look as they should on a range of different channels and measure
your impact in one place.

With this module you can connect your Drupal website to Story Chief.
After the simple setup is completed you will be able to publish your articles
from Story Chief straight to you Drupal website.

*  For a full description of the module, visit the project page:
   https://www.drupal.org/project/storychief

*  To submit bug reports and feature suggestions, or to track changes:
   https://www.drupal.org/project/issues/storychief


REQUIREMENTS
------------

No special requirements


INSTALLATION
------------

*  Install as you would normally install a contributed Drupal module. Visit:
   https://www.drupal.org/node/1897420 for further information.


CONFIGURATION
-------------

1. Direct your browser to admin/config/services/storychief to configure the
   module.

2. You will need to put in your Story Chief encryption key.
   If you do not have a Story Chief account, go to
   [https://storychief.io]([https://storychief.io) and sign up for a new
   account. Once you have set up your account and are logged in, visit:
   Channels -> Drupal -> Add destination to obtain an encryption key.

3. Copy your newly created Encryption key and go to the
   [Storychief config](http://example.com/admin/config/services/storychief)
   page in your Drupal site and paste it into the encryption key field.

4. Copy the Webook url and go to the configuration of your drupal destination
   at your Story Chief account.
   Past the Webook url into the Drupal url field and press 'Save configuration'

5. Choose a node type where Story Chief must save stories to

6. Last step! Map the Story Chief Story fields to your node fields
