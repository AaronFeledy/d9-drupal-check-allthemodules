INTRODUCTION
------------

This module(Amazing-forms) provides a custom form in modal using Drupal's FormBuilder and AJAX API.
Amazing-forms module provide a url(../amazing-forms/demo),once you hit the url, welcome page of the form will open.
There is a button link(click here to open form), once you click on button, Form will open on modal popup.
